A few natureme.com.au FAQs

Do you accept cash payment?

For the security of our customers and drivers we do not accept cash payment for jobs.

What's Eco-Cleaning?

Eco-cleaning is cleaning your home with green products and ingredients coming straight from nature. Eco cleaning will help you have a healthier, safer home, increasing air quality and having a purer environment.

What are the payment options?

Currently we accept secure online payments via debit/credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Amex).

Does the Eco-Cleanner bring cleanning supplies?

Yes, the eco-cleaner will bring with him green solutions for your home, however, for hygiene purposes, you will have to provide your own vacuum cleaner and mop.
